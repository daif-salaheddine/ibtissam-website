Professional Identity

"I have always struggled with accepting things simply because that is the way they are, especially in education & L&D, where the potential to do better is so often visible, and so often ignored."

Ibtissam Daif is an educator, learning designer, and workforce development consultant with over a decade of international expertise spanning Morocco and the United States.

Grounded in the American Liberal Arts model and trained in Boston's innovation ecosystem, she works at the intersection of curriculum architecture, business strategy, and human performance, translating complex organizational challenges into coherent, outcome-driven programs.

She designs and delivers natively in English, French, and Arabic for corporate, academic, and civil society clients across North Africa, the US, and Europe.
